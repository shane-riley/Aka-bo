Rules
The board is arranged with a series of rows and columns
A player can place a game piece in any column on the board with an open space
If there is already a game piece in that column, the new game piece will stack on top of the old one
A player wins by connecting 4 game pieces in a row
Connected game pieces can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
Game pieces are placed one at a time, back and forth between the two players
